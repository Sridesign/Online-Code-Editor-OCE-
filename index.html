Our Story

About Us

We are students of MPC College, and we built this project for students and teachers who want a simpler way to learn and teach web development.

This platform removes all the friction — no IDE to install, no environment to configure. Open a browser, write your code, and see the output immediately. Teachers can demonstrate concepts live; students can experiment freely on any device.

OCE is fully compatible with Windows, Linux, macOS and Android. Our goal is to lower the cost and complexity of learning web programming for everyone.
